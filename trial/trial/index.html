ABOUT US


We are a group of youngsters looking to render selfless service to the society in all the ways possible. Striving hard to be the better version of us everyday! Best environment to explore our leadership skills and mould our personality from within. We encourage volunteers to realise their role in contributing to a better nation by giving back to the society. A great platform for those who are passionate towards social service.
Trying to be the better version of us everyday!

Our Main Goals are:


  1. Collect and Distribute excess Food left in Marriages,Functions and in our vicinity.
  2. To establish a Book Bank with our old textbooks, Novels, Magazines.
  3. To conduct Awarenesses programs in school colleges on various issues to empower the students.
